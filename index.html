Jeromy in the Media

Every form of transportation is legitimate.

Published: July 26, 2025

The bike lane debate has taken over much of the current city council’s time and not much has been decided. This harms Calgarians. Jeromy is proposing tying funding to usage, all forms of transportation are legitimate and all need to be allowed to succeed where the demand is. 

“When we tie funding to usage, we take the guesswork and the gamesmanship out of it…Up until now we’ve had the sort of cars versus bikes debate and I think the way to break that logjam is to just acknowledge that every single form of transportation is legitimate.”

Getting around should not be a risk. With his 30-point transportation plan, Jeromy will ensure that Calgarians have the right, ability and access to movement, from public transit safety, snow and ice clearing, low-income transit passes and the creation of a real-time construction map.
